custom-volume-and-level-codes

Within the default configuration, the volume and level codes are simple integers. It is possible to create project specific codes and names as shown below for volumes and levels.

Equipment Tags
Projects contain multiple identical (equipment) type_tag's. bdns_tag's and instance_tag's are unique.
