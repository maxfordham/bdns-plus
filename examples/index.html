no-config-multi-volume

With no project configuration levels and volumes are represented as integer numbers. The available codes are:

  • Levels -10 -> 90
  • Volumes 1 -> 9
Equipment Tags
Projects contain multiple identical (equipment) type_tag's. bdns_tag's and instance_tag's are unique.
